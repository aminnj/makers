collect.py loops through all good stocks and saves sentiment data from market prophit.
turns out their "free/test" api key works for scraping thousands of stocks, so that's good.
http://developer.marketprophit.com/#market-prophit-sentiment, make sure to keep the username up to date by looking at their curl command
and editing the username in the script (maybe they change it every day or something)



reduce.py is an unfinished attempt to deal with empty entries


sentiment.tar.gz has sentiment data for 1.5k stocks from 2013-01-01 to 2015-10-04
